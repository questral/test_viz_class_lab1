Discoveries & Insights

Summary

There are a lot of analyses that I wasn't able to make because of my unfamiliarity with Vega-Lite, but what I was able to find was mostly time based. The accidents in this data set are heavily influenced by time: Time of day, month of the year, and of course yearly trends. Overall, sex and age seemed to have little bearing so far. Fentanyl, heroin, and cocaine are the most common drugs in general. There was also a bit of history revealed in the last graph that indicates some possible racial correlation with carfentanil. In future analysis, I would be interested to see how specific drugs are used across different subgroups.
